What we stand for?

V studio is a platform where we combine digital and traditional art and create a solution or product as per clients requirement. As of now V studio is a single owned platform.It is own by Andrea vlad.

Who am i?

I am a Visual Artist, Graphic & Communication Designer currently based in Copenhagen. With a BA in Communication Design & Media (School of Design & Business - KEA), a BA in Graphic Arts from University of Art and Design (Cluj-Napoca, Romania), and an AP in Multimedia Design from (KEA, Copenhagen), I developed an ample understanding of Visual Communication within analogue and digital media.
Having a focus on aesthetics, function, and experimentation, my approach could be described as a hybrid between art and design, with a strong interest in culture, technology, and critical thinking.
I work with a multidisciplinary approach that allows me to move, both conceptually and practically, between different disciplines and visualisation practices. My aspiration is to apply visual thinking and design methodologies through artistic practices to explore and solve complex issues in contemporary world and culture.
